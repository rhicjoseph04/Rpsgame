Rock Paper Scissors Game

Game Instructions

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game! The objective is to be the first player to win 3 games.

Click on the buttons for Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your move. The computer will make its choice as well.

Here are the rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.

After each round, the result will be displayed. If you win a round, you'll score a point. The first player to reach 3 points wins the game!

Click "Reset Button" to restart the game and try to beat the computer.
